FEATURES OF THE SERENGETI

Drive thru and feed exotic and domestic animals from around the World. Most are eagerly awaiting and will come right up to your car. Be sure and get a bucket of feed when you check in.

As you drive along our mile and a half of winding, scenic trails, don't miss out on Lenure Island. These little guys are most active early mornings and late evenings. During the heat of the day they like to hang out in their house which has a vented floor to keep them cool.

Halfway thru the tour you will find Roo and his mates. This enclosure has Kangaroos, Cave Nyala and other small animals.
