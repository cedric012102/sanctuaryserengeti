Rules of the Park


The rules of the park are to be followed at all times. These rules are put in place to keep you safe, as well as the animals.

1. We love our pets as much as you love yours, but under federal laws we cannot allow pets or service animals into the safari. No personal pets are permitted on the premesis.

2. Remain in your vehicle at all times.

3. No convertables or soft tops Convertable-top vehicles, or vehicles without a roof and windows are not allowed inside the park. This also includes jeeps without the proper siding and roll-up windows.

4. No riding in the beds of a truck All customers entering the park must stay inside the cab of their vehicle.

5. You must stay on the one-way roads at all times. The animals eat the grass! Please stay on the road.

6. Do not hand feed the animals for the safety of you, your car, and the animals, do NOT hand feed. Toss the feed on the ground.

7. No Motorcycles or ATVs No motorcycles or ATV's are allowed on the property at any time.
